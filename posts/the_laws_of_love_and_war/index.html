The Laws of Love and War (Cover) - The Glorious Sons

2020-02-24

More Glorious Sons! The guitar part for this song is a bit more technical than anything I’ve uploaded so far, I think I did an okay job of it! I can’t stop playing songs by this band, I think the writing generally suits a one-man cover and they’re great fun to learn. I’ll make sure my next cover is of a different band though.

This song is also (very) high in my register. I used to always stick to songs that let me sing in my lower register where I feel the most comfortable, and would even transpose songs down if they felt too high to me. These days I’m a bit more at home in my head voice, although it’s still outside of the norm for me. I think the rule is if I’m having fun singing it then hopefully having a go will push my boundaries a bit.
