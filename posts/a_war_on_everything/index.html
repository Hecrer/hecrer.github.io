A War On Everything (Cover) - The Glorious Sons

2020-02-05

The Glorious Sons are a rock band from Canada, and they were the main soundtrack to the first roadtrip me and some friends took around central Europe. After that we were lucky enough to catch them at a (free!) gig in London, which cemented my love for the band as they swung from the rafters and belted out tune after tune, the energy and heart of their performance makes it easy to feel a connection to the band live and get lost in the moment.

I’ve seen them a few times since, including on their cycle for A War On Everything (album). Each time they blow me away all over again. This song is one of my favourites from the latest album, so I threw together a quick and dirty cover.
