I Will Follow You Into the Dark (Cover) - Death Cab For Cutie

2020-09-10

Death is a difficult topic to approach, and many artists have their own take on dealing with the loss of their loved ones. This song resonated with me a lot when I first heard it some ten years ago, and it’s one I revisit every so often. It’s also probably one of the most covered tracks by Death Cab For Cutie, and here I am adding to the list.
